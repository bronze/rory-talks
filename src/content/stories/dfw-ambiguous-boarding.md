title: "Dallas Fort Worth's Ambiguous Boarding Gate"
description: "Courtney Moore, head of behavioural science at Dallas Fort Worth airport, observed that eight passengers who queue too early at the gate trigger a cascade: everyone else joins the queue out of FOMO, turning the last 25 minutes before boarding into standing in line rather than sitting comfortably. DFW's solution is to make the exact boarding location ambiguous so no queue can form prematurely. It is another example of changing the quality of waiting time without altering its duration."
