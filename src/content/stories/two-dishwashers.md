title: "Two Dishwashers"
description: "Rory's suggestion: install two dishwashers and never unload one again. Use the clean dishwasher as a cupboard; when the dirty one fills up, run it, and now it is the clean cupboard. The idea sounds mad until explained, at which point it is obvious — illustrating how many genuinely useful innovations are invisible to pre-rationalisation but obvious in retrospect, and that the opposite of a good idea (one dishwasher) is often another good idea (two dishwashers)."
