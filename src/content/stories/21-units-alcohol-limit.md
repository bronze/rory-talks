title: "The 21-Units Alcohol Limit Fails by Design"
description: "Public health guidance sets a quantitative weekly alcohol limit — say 21 units — which fails on three counts: it is a soft numerical limit rather than a binary rule, so people routinely drift over it without feeling they've done something clearly wrong; it requires you to stop drinking when you're already drunk; and it cannot become socially contagious because all your friends would need to drink at exactly the same rate. Binary rules, like \"don't drink on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday,\" work far better."
