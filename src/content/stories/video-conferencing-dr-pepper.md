description: "In 2019, flying to Frankfurt for a two-hour meeting was the default (Coca-Cola) and video conferencing was Dr Pepper — an outlier you had to justify. Post-pandemic, the social norms have inverted: video conferencing is now Coke and flying over is Dr Pepper. Rory uses the drinks analogy to show how rapidly social norms can shift, making behaviours that once required explanation suddenly require justification in reverse."
