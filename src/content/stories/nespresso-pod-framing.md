title: "Nespresso's Psychological Arbitrage"
description: "The same caffeine in a Nespresso pod would cost around £30–35 if sold in a jar like instant coffee — obviously absurd. But sold as a 40p pod, the reference frame shifts to Starbucks, making the machine feel like it is practically saving you money at £2.80 per cup. This is Rory's definitive example of psychological arbitrage: finding latent value in an existing product by changing its frame of reference rather than its physical form."
