title: "Henry Ford and the Two-Day Weekend"
description: "Henry Ford was instrumental in establishing the two-day weekend for his workers — not purely out of altruism, but because he realised that with a two-day weekend it becomes worth buying a car. Rather than asking how to extract maximum labour from workers, he asked a different question: how do we create enough leisure in wider society to make car ownership desirable? Rory uses this to argue for four weeks of guaranteed paid US vacation, on the grounds that it would inject cash into the discretionary economy the travel industry depends on."
