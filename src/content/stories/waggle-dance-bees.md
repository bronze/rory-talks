description: "About 20% of bees ignore the waggle dance — the evolved communication system that directs foragers to known pollen sources — and fly off at random instead. Bee scientists were horrified until they modelled it as a complex system: the random explorers are the hive's hedge against the future, finding new sources when old ones fail, and the same explore-exploit trade-off appears in AI and all dynamic systems. An organisation that achieves 100% waggle-dance compliance becomes over-optimised on the past and eventually starves."
