description: "Retailers and spirits manufacturers placed a cocktail pod — ice, mixers, lemons and limes — at the end of the supermarket aisle, at the \"what shall I drink tonight?\" decision point, rather than requiring shoppers to double back for mixers. Sales of spirits in those stores rose by around 8%, entirely because path dependency had been eliminated rather than because of any change in price or preference."
