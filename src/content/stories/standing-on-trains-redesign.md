title: "Redesigning Trains for Standing Passengers"
description: "Rory asks why people dislike standing on commuter trains and unpacks multiple causes: inability to use a phone while holding on, no view, no table, no cup holder — standing passengers get nothing while seated passengers get everything. He proposes redistributing the benefits (window-side bum-rests, cup holders, USB chargers, a view) so that standing becomes a genuine choice rather than a pure deprivation. This lets adaptive preference formation work: people reframe the compromise as a preference, reducing net regret and creating utility from nothing."
