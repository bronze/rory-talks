title: "Fast Food Pre-Order Screens and the Quality of Waiting"
description: "The time spent queuing to place your order at a fast food counter is purely negative, but the moment you have placed it the remaining wait is reframed as 'time devoted to the quality preparation of my meal' and becomes far more tolerable. Digital ordering screens do not shorten the wait; they move the customer past the reframing point sooner. This is Rory's illustration that the quality of time is a design variable independent of its duration."
