title: "Electric Car Charger: Getting the Order Right"
description: "The UK government subsidises home EV charging points, but requires proof of owning an electric car before the subsidy can be claimed. Rory argues this is the wrong sequence: if you can get someone to install a charger on their wall for £100, they will feel ridiculous buying a diesel next — the commitment to the charger drives the car purchase, not the other way around. The story is his example of psychological sequencing: the order in which things happen determines behaviour more than the incentives attached to any individual step."
