title: "The Heathrow Pod Parking System"
description: "The driverless pod cars at Heathrow Terminal 5 connect a car park about a mile from the terminal, yet they often cost more than short-stay parking right next to the airport — and business travellers still prefer them. Colleagues in their 60s, when told the pod is full and they have been upgraded to short-stay for free, secretly feel disappointed because they were looking forward to the ride. The example shows how unmeasured experiential value (fun) easily outweighs objectively superior utility (proximity)."
